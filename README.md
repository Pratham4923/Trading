🌌 Quantum Titan | Trading Terminal v2.0
Quantum Titan is a high-performance, aesthetically driven trading journal and analytics dashboard. Built for traders who demand both utility and luxury, it combines deep glassmorphism aesthetics with real-time equity tracking and strategy breakdown.

✨ Key Features
📈 Dynamic Equity Flow: A real-time line chart that tracks your account growth with every trade.

🧠 Strategy Breakdown: A specialized bar chart that calculates P/L per strategy (SMC, Price Action, etc.), showing you exactly what's making you money.

🔥 Session Heatmap: A visual monthly calendar that highlights your winning and losing days with color-coded intensity.

💎 Hybrid Logic: A "Smart Strategy" input that lets you choose from famous presets or type in your own custom edge.

⚡ Success Physics: Custom-engineered animations including spring-bounce modals, success-pulse buttons, and staggered entrance effects.

🗑️ Real-time Management: Fully interactive history log with instant delete capabilities and automatic data recalculation.

🛠️ The Technology Stack
Core: HTML5 / CSS3 (Custom Properties & Keyframe Engine)

Dynamics: JavaScript (ES6+)

Engine: Chart.js 4.0 (Performance Optimized)

Fonts: Plus Jakarta Sans (800 weight for that "Elite" feel)

Storage: LocalStorage (Zero-server setup, your data stays on your machine)

🚀 Quick Start
Clone the file: Save the code as index.html.

Launch: Open the file in any modern browser (Chrome/Edge recommended for best animation performance).

Deploy: To use on mobile or across devices, simply host it on GitHub Pages or Vercel for free.

📸 Interface Guide
1. Adding a Trade
Click the floating + button. The background will melt into a 15px blur, and the command center will eject. Fill in your stats and hit Save.

2. Strategy Analysis
The terminal automatically groups your trades. If you want to see how "Price Action" compares to "SMC," ensure you select/type those names in the strategy field.

3. Deleting Data
Made a mistake? Just hit the 🗑️ icon in the history log. The terminal will immediately update your equity curve and heatmap.

🛡️ Data Privacy
Quantum Titan is a client-side application.

[!IMPORTANT]
Your trading data is stored in your browser's LocalStorage. It is never uploaded to a cloud or shared with third parties. Clearing your browser cache will erase your data unless you've backed it up.
